Hello! I'm a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Cornell Universityüß∏.

Before joining Cornell, I received my Master's degree from UC San Diegoüåä, where I worked with Prof. Xiaolong Wang, and my Bachelor's degree from Nanjing Universityüê≥.

My research goal is to endow embodied agents with versatile skills by learning efficiently and continually in the physical world. Toward that end, my research is focused on data-driven and model-based decision-making for robotics, especially the following directions:

  • World models/Model-based reinforcement learning for sample-efficient learning,
  • Hierarchical learning/planning/control for solving long-horizon tasks, and
  • Grounding foundation models(LLMs/VLMs) to handle complex robotic tasks.
